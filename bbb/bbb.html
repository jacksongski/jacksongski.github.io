Big Buff Badgers App

This is the group project I worked on in my Software Engineering class. It served as a gym buddy finder in the style of a dating app. A user could enter their profile information, upload images, set preferences, and match with other users.

Technologies used:

  • FastAPI (Python)

  • Google Cloud Platform
